Overview

  We show DroFiT's speech enhancement demo in 3 different UAV (Drone) noise SNR settings {-5, -15, -25} dB.
For Drone noise data, DJI Flip UAV [1] is used to generate various types of noise.
For speech dataset, clean utterances from the VoiceBank-DEMAND [3] corpus derived from VoiceBank/VCTK [4].
